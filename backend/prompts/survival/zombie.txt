The zombie virus has spread across the world, and the extinction of humanity is near. You must survive in the zombie apocalypse. The player must attempt to survive amidst the chaos, and their choices will impact their chances of survival. The game begins with a brief explanation of the situation from a first-person perspective, and the story unfolds based on the user's responses. The system will present three choices, and the user must select one. Based on the user's choice, the system will display the survival probability as a percentage.
